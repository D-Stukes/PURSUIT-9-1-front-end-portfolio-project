About The Developer

Introducing Diane Stukes....your Full Stack Web Developer

Why I Chose Web Development

I love the having the ability to create a medium that I use and depend on in my every day life. Whether is it is shopping, paying bills, looking for information or just exploring new ideas. Like practically everyone else, I web pages are an integral part of my life. So why not innovate instead of just relying on the works of others?

Why Chose To Be A Full Stack Developer

I enjoy making things beautiful. Front-end development allows for exciting ways to present information on my web pages. Growing up, I was a natural artist, able to draw and paint credible works. During my career path, I always found myself creating beautiful, artistic presentations. Enjoying this creative opportunity as a Web Developer is one I must embrace!

Still, though I love "pretty", I also enjoy "power". There is another awesome side to web development. Being able to deliver and receive data, update it, store it is just as important as presenting. This a must-have power for me as a web developer!
